Frequently Asked Questions

🖼️ What image formats are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, WebP, and BMP.

🔐 Is my image safe?

Yes, all processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to servers.

📱 Can I use this tool on mobile?

Absolutely! The tool is fully responsive for smartphones and tablets.

📏 Can I rotate by any angle?

Yes, use the custom angle input to rotate by any degree between -360° and 360°.
